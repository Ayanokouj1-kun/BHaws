# BHaws Management and Monitoring System: Functionality Summary

This document provides a clear and concise overview of the features and capabilities of the **BHaws Management System**, designed to streamline boarding house operations.

### 1. Centralized Dashboard & Analytics
*   **Real-time KPI Tracking**: Instantly view the number of active boarders, current occupancy rates, and net cash flow.
*   **Visual Data Analytics**: Interactive charts showing monthly revenue (Income vs. Expenses) and payment status distributions.
*   **Smart Alerts**: Automatic notifications for urgent maintenance requests and overdue payments to ensure immediate attention.

### 2. Comprehensive Boarder Management
*   **Profile Tracking**: Maintain detailed records for every boarder, including contact information and emergency details.
*   **Occupancy Mapping**: Effortlessly assign boarders to specific rooms and beds.
*   **Personalized Boarder Portals**: A dedicated view for boarders to check their room details, stay updated with announcements, and track their payment history.

### 3. Room & Inventory Control
*   **Floor-by-Floor Management**: Organize and view rooms by floor levels.
*   **Bed-Level Availability**: Monitor the status of every single bed (Occupied, Available, or Under Maintenance) to prevent overbooking.
*   **Detailed Room Specs**: Store specific information about room pricing, capacity, and amenities.

### 4. Financial Transaction Tracking
*   **Payment Monitoring**: Log and track boarder payments with statuses like Paid, Pending, or Overdue.
*   **Expense Logging**: Record operational costs such as electricity, water, and repairs to calculate accurate profit margins.
*   **Automated Due Dates**: The system identifies and flags overdue accounts automatically.

### 5. Seamless Digital Payments
*   **GCash Integration**: A built-in payment portal that displays the administrator's GCash QR code for easy scanning.
*   **Quick Copy Feature**: Allows boarders to copy account numbers with a single click to avoid manual entry errors.

### 6. Maintenance Request System
*   **Issue Reporting**: A structured way for boarders or staff to report maintenance problems (e.g., broken faucets, electrical issues).
*   **Priority Ranking**: Categorize tasks by urgency (Urgent, High, Medium, Low) to prioritize critical repairs.
*   **Status Workflow**: Track repairs from "Open" to "In Progress" and finally "Resolved."

### 7. Accountability & Reporting
*   **Audit Logs**: A transparent record of every action taken in the system, identifying who made changes and when.
*   **Data Export**: Generate and download reports for financial auditing or occupancy history.

### 8. System Administration & Security
*   **Role-Based Access**: Specialized permissions for Admins (full control), Staff (operational access), and Boarders (view-only/limited interaction).
*   **Global Announcements**: Broadcast important updates or house rules directly to all users via the dashboard.
*   **System Configuration**: Easily update house branding, contact details, and payment information in one central location.
